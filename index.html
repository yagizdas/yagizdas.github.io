About Me

I am Yagiz, a senior year Electronics and Communications Engineering student at Istanbul Technical University. I am mainly working on AI/ML and Cloud. I previously interned at Microsoft and Airties.

Currently, I am working as a Data Science Project Student at BSH Home Appliances Group, a Bosch subsidiary, where I work on developing Agentic AI-based solutions for workflows across departments. I am also participating as an Undergraduate Machine Learning Research Assistant at Multimedia Signal Processing and Pattern Recognition Research Group at Istanbul Technical University.

My research focuses on transformer-based crowd counting models for autonomous driving.

I work with technoloies such as

  • Agentic AI
  • Computer Vision
  • AWS
  • Language Models (SLMs/LLMs)
